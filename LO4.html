Portfolio Design

I did a user test on my portfolio and development project with two people. Jo-An gave me some helpful feedback. She liked my portfolio idea but suggested I make my name and message stand out more instead of the flower design, which made it feel like a flower shop. So, I decided to simplify the design and blur some elements to keep the focus on my name and message. For the development project, Jo-An said the order of the pages didn’t make sense. For example, the countdown should come after the introduction, not before. I’ll adjust the order to make the flow more logical.
